Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 27 June 2026

CAT RC Mastery Elite ("the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information the App collects and how it is used.

1. Information We Collect

CAT RC Mastery Elite is designed to work primarily offline.

The App does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information, including:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Location
  • Contacts
  • Photos
  • Files
  • Messages
  • Camera or microphone data

The App does not require user registration or account creation.

2. Reading Progress

Your reading progress, bookmarks, vocabulary progress, statistics, weekly goals, achievements, and app preferences are stored only on your device.

This information never leaves your device and is not transmitted to the developer.

3. Purchases

The App offers a one-time premium unlock through Google Play Billing.

Payment processing is handled entirely by Google. The developer does not receive or store your payment card details, bank account information, or payment credentials.

4. Internet Usage

The App works offline for its core functionality.

Internet access may be used only for:

  • Verifying purchases through Google Play
  • Downloading app updates from Google Play

5. Third-Party Services

The App uses:

  • Google Play Billing

The App does not include:

  • Advertising SDKs
  • Analytics SDKs
  • User tracking technologies
  • Crash reporting services

6. Data Sharing

The developer does not sell, rent, or share any personal information because the App does not collect personal information.

7. Children's Privacy

The App is intended for students preparing for competitive examinations such as CAT, XAT, SNAP, IIFT, GMAT, and similar aptitude tests.

The App is not specifically directed toward children under the age of 13, and no personal information is knowingly collected from children.

8. Data Security

Since user progress is stored locally on the device, users are responsible for securing access to their own devices.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the Effective Date at the top of this page.
